About Me

How I became interested in photography

Thank you for visiting my presence on the web! This section is here for you to learn a little more about me and my work. So, with that said, following is a little bit about me!

I am a Sydney, Australia based Landscape and Nature Photographer.

I love taking photos from behind the lens and it's then I get to see the world, nature, people, events in a whole new way. I get a massive insspiration of nature and it would easily be the driving force behind my photography, creativity.

I have picked up my first DSLR at the end of 2010, originally I was trying to get better photo compared to my point and shoot camera. I soon found out I was taking more photos of everything else as well, and thus a completely new hobby was born much to my surprise.

I taught myself through photography books, online tutorials, great friends and most importantly trial and error. Im always learning, taking on board every shoot I go on, always striving to better my craft in anyway I can.

Personal Interests
